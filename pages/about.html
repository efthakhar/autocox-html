About Us

AutoCox enters Bangladesh’s competitive car reselling market with a mission to create a meaningful impact. Unlike typical Japanese reconditioned car resellers, we strive to be the country’s first online car seller with a physical presence.

Our journey began with the sale of four Japanese reconditioned cars, a milestone that strengthened our foundation and provided valuable insights. We prioritize a robust organizational structure and unwavering professionalism, ensuring excellence in every aspect of our operations. With a clear vision and dedication, AutoCox is set to redefine the car reselling industry by blending online convenience with a trusted physical presence.
